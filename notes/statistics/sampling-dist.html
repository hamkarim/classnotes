ជាទូទៅ របាយរបស់អថេរចៃដន្យមួយ គឺជា របាយរបស់សំណាក ហើយរបាយរបស់អថេរចៃដន្យមួយចំនួនមានភាពស្មុគ្រស្មាញ ទោះបីជាមាន របាយប៉ូពុយឡាស្យុងខ្លួនវា ល្អយ៉ាងណាក៏ដោយ (ឧទាហរណ៍ របាយនរម៉ាល់)។ ដូច្នេះ អថេរបែបនេះ គឺជាបរិមាណគម្រូពិសេស នៅពេលណាគេចាត់ទុកវាជាអថេរចៃដន្យ។


ការកំណត់បានសំណាកដោយចៃដន្យ

  1. A population consists of the totality of the observations with which we are concerned. (Population=Probability Distribution)
  2. A sample is a subset of a population.

សម្គាល់.

  • Each observation in a population is a value of a random variable $X$ having some probability distribution $f(x)$.
  • To eliminate bias in the sampling procedure, we select a random sample in the sense that the observations are made independently and at random.
  • The random sample of size $n$ is: $X_1, X_2, \cdots , X_n$
    • It consists of n observations selected independently and randomly from the population.

តម្លៃស្ថិតិសំខាន់ៗមួយចំនួន

  1. Any function of the random sample $X_1, X_2, \cdots, X_n$ is called a statistic.
  2. If $X_1, X_2, \cdots, X_n$ represents a random sample of size $n$, then the sample mean is defined to be the statistic: \[ \overline{X}=\frac{X_{1}+X_{2}+\cdots+X_{n}}{n} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i}}{n} \]

តាមនិយមន័យខាងលើ គេអាចកំណត់សរសេរបានបម្រែបម្រួល ដូចខាងក្រោម៖ $$ S^{2}=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i}^{2}-n \overline{X}^{2}}{n-1}=\frac{n \sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i}^{2}-\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i}\right)^{2}}{n(n-1)} $$

សម្គាល់.

  • $\overline{X}$ is a statistic because it is a function of the random sample $X_1, X_2, \cdots, X_n$
  • $\overline{X}$ has same unit of $X_1, X_2, \cdots, X_n$.
  • $\overline{X}$ measures the central tendency in the sample (location).

(Variability in the Sample)

If \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) represents a random sample of size \(n,\) then the sample variance is defined to be the statistic: $$ S^{2}=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left(X_{i}-\overline{X}\right)^{2}}{n-1} \qquad (\text {unit})^{2} $$

សម្គាល់.

  • $S^2$ is a statistic because it is a function of the random sample $X_1, X_2, \cdots, X_n$
  • $S^2$ measures the variability in the sample.

(Variability in the Sample)

The sample standard deviation is defined to be the statistic: $$ S=\sqrt{S^{2}}=\sqrt{\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left(X_{i}-\bar{X}\right)^{2}}{n-1}} \qquad \text { (unit) } $$

Sampling distribution

(Sampling distribution)

The probability distribution of a statistic is called a sampling distribution. The sampling distribution of a given population is the distribution of frequencies of a range of different outcomes that could possibly occur for a statistic of a population.

សម្គាល់.

  • A sampling distribution is a statistic that is arrived out through repeated sampling from a larger population.
  • It describes a range of possible outcomes that of a statistic, such as the mean or mode of some variable, as it truly exists a population.
  • The majority of data analyzed by researchers are actually drawn from samples, and not populations.
